Academic Excellence

JECRC University offers a wide array of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across disciplines such as:

  • Engineering & Technology (B.Tech, M.Tech)
  • Computer Applications (BCA, MCA)
  • Management (BBA, MBA)
  • Sciences (B.Sc, M.Sc)
  • Humanities & Social Sciences
  • Design, Law, Agriculture, and Journalism

The curriculum is designed with input from industry experts and is continuously updated to reflect emerging trends in technology and management. The university places a strong emphasis on outcome-based education (OBE), hands-on projects, and skill-based learning.

Infrastructure

Spread across a sprawling 32-acre Wi-Fi-enabled campus, JECRC University features:

  • State-of-the-art laboratories and research centers
  • Digital libraries and smart classrooms
  • Hostel accommodation with modern amenities
  • Sports and fitness facilities
  • 24/7 medical assistance and campus security

Innovation & Research

JECRC has a dedicated JECRC Incubation Centre (JIC) to foster innovation and entrepreneurship among students. It has collaborated with government initiatives like Atal Innovation Mission, Start-up India, and DST Rajasthan to support student-led startups. Research at JECRC spans across emerging technologies like AI, Data Science, IoT, Blockchain, Cybersecurity, and Renewable Energy.

Industry Tie-ups & Placements

The university has built robust partnerships with companies like Infosys, TCS, Wipro, IBM, Accenture, and many more. Its Career Development Cell (CDC) conducts regular training, placement drives, and industrial interactions. JECRC consistently achieves over 90% placement in core and emerging sectors.

Cultural & Social Initiatives

JECRC believes in nurturing well-rounded individuals. The campus hosts numerous cultural festivals, technical competitions, hackathons, blood donation camps, NSS drives, and community outreach programs. The annual fest, JU Rhythm, is one of Rajasthan's biggest student festivals.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To become a renowned center of excellence in education, research, innovation, and entrepreneurship that produces socially responsible global citizens.
Mission: To provide quality education through a dynamic curriculum, collaborative learning, and industry engagement.
